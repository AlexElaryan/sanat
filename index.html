About Us

Sanat KG LLC, founded in 2021 in Kyrgyzstan, specializes in the wholesale trade of a wide range of products for civilian use, including pumping equipment. We collaborate with leading suppliers from China and Southeast Asia and organize deliveries to Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Azerbaijan, and Turkey.
